How To Play

Simple Rules,
Endless Fun

Learn the game in under a minute. Master it over a lifetime.

1

Set Up

Place the Keep It Up table on any flat surface — sand, grass, pavement, you name it. Two teams of two stand on opposite sides.

2

Kick & Rally

Serve the ball by kicking it onto the table. The other team has to kick it back on. Use any part of your body — except your hands!

3

Keep It Up!

Rally back and forth until someone misses. First team to the winning score takes the crown. Simple to learn, impossible to put down.
