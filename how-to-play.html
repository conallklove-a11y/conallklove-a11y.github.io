How To Play

Simple Rules,
Endless Fun

Learn the game in under a minute. Master it over a lifetime.

1

Set Up

Place the Keep It Up table on any flat surface — sand, grass, pavement, you name it. Two teams of two stand on opposite sides.

2

Kick & Rally

Serve the ball by kicking it onto the table. The other team has to kick it back on. Use any part of your body — except your hands!

3

Keep It Up!

Rally back and forth until someone misses. First team to the winning score takes the crown. Simple to learn, impossible to put down.

The Rules

Know the Game

Everything you need to play like a pro from day one.

👥

Teams

2 teams of 2 players each. Stand on opposite sides of the table and defend your side.

⚽

No Hands

Feet, knees, chest — anything goes except your hands. Keep it soccer-style!

🏆

Scoring

You score when the opposing team fails to return the ball. First to 21 points wins the set.

🔄

Serve Rotation

Serve rotates after each point. Every player on both teams gets their turn to serve.

📐

Boundaries

The ball must land on the table surface. Edge shots count — rim shots are fair game!

🎯

Win by 2

At 20-20, you must win by 2 points. Play continues until one team pulls ahead.
