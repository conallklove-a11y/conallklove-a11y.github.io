Learn in 30 Seconds

Three steps. That's it.

1

Set Up

Place the table on any flat surface. Teams of two stand on opposite sides.

2

Kick & Rally

Kick the ball onto the table. The other team kicks it back. No hands allowed!

3

Keep It Up!

Rally until someone misses. First to the winning score takes the crown.
